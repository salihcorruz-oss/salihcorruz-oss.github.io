Privacy Policy — How Many Days?

Effective date: May 24, 2026

How Many Days? is designed to work primarily offline. Developer: Salih Çörüz (ClickWise).

1. Data we collect

Personal event data you enter (titles, dates, optional notes, optional images) and app settings stored on your device.

2. Where data is stored

Data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a user account server for core app data.

3. Advertising

Free users may see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data per Google's Privacy Policy. Pro (lifetime) users do not see ads.

4. In-app purchases

Purchases are processed by Google Play. We receive purchase status to unlock Pro features.

5. Internet usage

Core features work offline. External links (e.g. Google Maps, Play Store) are handled by those services.

6. Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is not shared except when you use system share actions or third-party services you open.

7. Your control

You can edit or delete your data in the app. Uninstalling removes local app data.
